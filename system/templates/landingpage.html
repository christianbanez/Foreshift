Are you on the right course?

By answering a few questions, you will know your chance to shift to another program.


Study right.

It can be challenging to live in college. You are entering maturity and taking on a lot of new experiences and obligations. It is more fruitful to acquire relevant knowledge and skills from a course that you really want.

Know yourself.

The right course that fits your interests, skills, or hobbies will let you learn more and enjoy the field you will be taking. Pay attention to every detail and see what strikes a chord with you

Save time.

Taking programs that are not aligned with what you want costs you time. If you select the wrong major, only to change courses midway, you will lose considerable time. Save your time for something that is right for you.
