About Us

Foreshift is a web application made by a group of researchers, in which, it uses machine learning to predict a student’s potential to shift to another course. The researchers aim to provide help in academic advising for college faculties and their students. This is to ensure that a student is on the right track for their selected career path.

The research started in 2022 by Information Technology students – namely Christian John Banez, Vea Therese Cruz, Ibanica Dionisio, and Princess Nicole Salvador.
